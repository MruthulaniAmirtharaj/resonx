About Us

What is RESONX 2K25?

RESONX 2K25 is a National Level Technical Symposium organized by the ECE Department, dedicated to fostering innovation and showcasing the latest in technology. Join us for a day of challenging technical events, fun-filled non-technical competitions, and a chance to engage with the future of electronics and communication.
